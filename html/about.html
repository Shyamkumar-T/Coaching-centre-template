The Elite Legacy

Founded in 2005, Elite Academy started with a vision to democratize high-quality education for competitive exams. What began as a small classroom of 20 students has now transformed into a national institution.

Our focus is not just on rote learning, but on building fundamental concepts. We believe every student has the potential to crack top exams given the right guidance and environment.
